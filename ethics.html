IT Ethics

In the tech field, it's important to stay ethical. That means protecting people's personal data, giving credit where it's due, and being responsible with how you use technology. Cybersecurity experts especially need to think about how their work affects others.

For example, ethical hackers help companies fix problems instead of causing them. Developers should not create apps that invade privacy or spread false information. It's also important to follow the law and respect others' digital work.

Source: NC State IT Ethics Guide
